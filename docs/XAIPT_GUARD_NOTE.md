# XAIPT-GUARD™
## Public Research Edition

> Human-Centered Execution Governance  
> For Irreversible Digital Actions.

---

# Overview

XAIPT-GUARD™ is a public research-oriented runtime governance project exploring how high-risk digital actions may benefit from:

- bounded execution
- authority visibility
- delayed finality
- review-aware interaction
- human-centered governance flows

The project focuses on the philosophy:

> “Human authority before irreversible execution.”

---

# Why XAIPT-GUARD Exists

Modern systems optimize for:

- speed
- automation
- frictionless execution
- instant finality

But irreversible digital actions increasingly face:

- scam pressure
- urgency attacks
- authority spoofing
- remote compromise
- forced execution behavior

XAIPT-GUARD explores whether:

- HOLD states
- secondary review
- authority topology
- audit visibility
- bounded delay

can become a meaningful governance layer for future digital systems.
