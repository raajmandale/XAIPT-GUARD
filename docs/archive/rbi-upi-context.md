# RBI / UPI Context

XAIPT-GUARD is not a policy claim and not a banking product.

It is a public research demonstrator aligned with a broader problem now visible across digital payment safety discussions: instant execution can become a fraud amplifier.

## Shared problem space

RBI/UPI fraud-protection directions and XAIPT-GUARD thinking overlap around:

- fraud friction
- trusted approvals
- cooling / delay concepts
- safer digital-payment behavior
- interruption before irreversible consequence

## Difference

| RBI / Payment Rails | XAIPT-GUARD Public Research |
|---|---|
| Bank / network level safeguards | Human-centered governance surface |
| Policy and operational controls | UX and architecture demonstrator |
| Payment-system specific | General irreversible-execution pattern |
| Institutional implementation | Public research abstraction |

## Position

XAIPT-GUARD does not claim ownership over policy direction. It is an independent research artifact exploring the same systemic pressure from a product and execution-governance lens.
